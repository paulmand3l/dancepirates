Show and Go

Begin by setting an expectation for what’s about to happen

We’re going to demo this move 5 times. At the end of those 5 times, you’re going to do it. Watch carefully.

Demo the move, changing orientations/directions and counting each time. Most importantly, SHUT UP while demoing. Let the students concentrate.

At the end of the 5 times, let them try it twice. They’re going to fail.

Rotate.

You all want to see it again? Ok, we’re going to show the move 3 more times.

Demo 3 or 4 more times. You should get some great ‘OHHHH’ sounds. Very satisfying. Let them try it again.

Rotate.

Walk students through the move. Point things out through questions: “Which foot am I spinning on?” “Where does my hand go?” If students have questions of their own, tease the question out into something they can observe, then demo the move. Let them try it more times.

Rotate.
