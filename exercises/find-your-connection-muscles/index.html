Find Your Connection Muscles all

by Nicole Trissell

  • Repeat this exercise with both arms every day until you find your arms relaxing more as you dance.
  • Focus your thoughts on how the muscles feel when doing the exercise, both which ones are toned and which ones are relaxed.

Exercise

Find yourself a doorway.

Roll your right shoulder solidly into its socket, drawing your right shoulder blade down your spine and engaging your lats.

Raise your right arm and stand close enough to the door that you can wrap your fingertips around the edge of the doorway with your elbow slightly bent.

Settle your hips back so that you are lightly hanging onto the doorway to stay standing.

Focus on your fingertips and your back, this is where most of the tone to hold you up should be. Try and relax the muscles in your arm as much as possible.

Hold this position for at least 30 seconds.

Repeat on the left side.

Tips

  • Be sure to maintain otherwise good posture while doing this. Keep your shoulders over your hips, your chest expanded and your head up.
