Spinning Practice all

Practice your balance and spinning technique all in one!

  • Each exercise should be done on both feet and in both directions, where applicable.
  • Turning is a skill, not a talent. Spend 5 minutes a day practicing these and you will get better.
  • Throughout, keep an emphasis on precision, not power or speed.

Exercise

Start with balancing on one foot for 30s - 1m.

Repeat on relevé (balancing on the ball of the foot).

From a small plie, launch onto relevé and hold balance.

From a small plie, launch into a quarter turn.

… half turn.

… full turn.

… double turn.

… triple turn.

Keep going until your students are in awe of your turning ability.

Tips

  • Engaging your [[core]] helps you maintain your [[axis]], which helps you maintain your balance. Maintain [[good posture]].
  • For best practice, attempt to end each turn on balance. That is, the turn should end–you should stop moving–while you’re still in relevé. Then after a moment’s pause, relax.
