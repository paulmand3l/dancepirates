What is Dance Pirates?

Dance Pirates is an open collaboration between blues and fusion instructors and dancers with the goal of making it easier and more accessible to learn how to dance, and to learn how to teach dance.

Best of all, you don’t need any account or membership to access our content, and it will always be free (as in grog).

How do I use this site?

Start by looking at our curriculum. There you’ll see the list of skills we think it’s important to learn to progress as a dancer. You can also check out our class plans or individual exercises.

How do I contribute?

E-mail captnjack@dancepirates.com with your best excuse for wanting to join the crew.
